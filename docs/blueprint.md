# **App Name**: Gharelu Upay Care

## Core Features:

- Remedy Explorer: Browse domestic remedies for common health issues like 'Ghutno ka dard' and 'Khansi' through interactive content cards.
- Smart Ailment Search: Quickly filter remedies using a top-mounted search bar that allows users to look up symptoms in Hindi or English.
- Vaidya AI Consultant: An AI advisor used as a tool to interpret symptom descriptions and suggest safe, traditional Ayurvedic home remedies.
- Favorites Manager: Save frequently used home remedies to a dedicated favorites tab for offline reference.
- Symptom Category Grid: Structured grid navigation to explore health areas such as digestion, respiratory care, and joint pain.
- Expert Information Portal: A deep-dive section under the 'वैद्य जी सलाह' tab for verifying safety information and ingredient uses.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background: A deep, serene Emerald Ayurvedic Green (#0E1513) chosen for its association with forest medicine and restorative peace.
- Primary Color: Warm Golden Amber (#E1AF31) used for headers and interactive elements to signify quality and traditional wellness.
- Accent: Burnt Terra-Cotta (#C83D23) for emphasis and high-priority action buttons, providing an organic, warm contrast.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Alegreya', a serif font with a classic literary feel, selected to lend credibility and an traditional touch to Hindi text.
- A vertical-scroll Single Page Application layout featuring a pinned top branding bar and a persistent bottom navigation with three primary sections: Home, Favorites, and Info.
- Fine-lined iconography rendered in Warm Gold, specifically designed to match the 'Amrit' aesthetic for navigation tabs.
- Smooth horizontal carousel transitions between remedy categories and subtle scale-up effects on tapping content cards.
